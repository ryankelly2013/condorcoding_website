About Condor

Condor is a computer science school designed to expose young students to computer science at an early age to give them an edge as they start their college career. Our goal is to cover similar material taught in most introductory and intermediate university computers science courses.

At Condor, we not only want to teach students how to code, but we want to teach them real world computer science skills at an early age. These skills will give students a leg up over their peers if they decide to major in computer science, engineering, or business in college.

With one-on-one sessions with Condor teachers, students ask questions and work through examples with the guidance of their teacher. Teachers can tailor each session to their student's specific needs.
